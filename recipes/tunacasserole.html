Tuna Casserole

Description

A classic Tuna Noodle Casserole recipe is an old-fashioned, easy dinner that has been loved for generations! Best of all, the family-friendly dish comes together in about 20 minutes. You can't beat the cheesy combination of tender noodles, canned tuna fish, and sweet peas tossed in a creamy sauce with crushed potato chips on top!

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • Condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Milk
  • Canned tuna
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Potato chips
  • Frozen peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles. Cook the noodles in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions, just until al dente. For wide egg noodles, this should only take about 5 minutes. Drain.
  2. Stir together milk and soup. In a large bowl, whisk together condensed cream of mushroom soup and milk until completely combined.
  3. Add remaining ingredients. Stir in the cooked noodles, tuna and peas. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Add topping. Transfer the noodle mixture to a 1 Â½-quart baking dish. Add grated cheese and sprinkle crushed potato chips on top.
  5. Bake. Bake the casserole uncovered in a 350 degree F oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the inside is hot and bubbly.
